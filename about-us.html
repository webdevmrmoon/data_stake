Our Growth Story

From Vision to Reality

2007

CBL Solutions is founded, focusing on custom integration solutions with a vision to simplify workflows and boost business efficiency.

2012

Expansion with Zeneth Solutions, specializing in healthcare integration, paving the way for industry- specific expertise.

2015

Launch of RFR IT, providing niche- agnostic staffing and skill development solutions for modern global business needs.

2018

ThinkSource emerges, adopting a platform- oriented approach as a key Boomi integration partner

2022

Founding of Dataflake, emphasizing data engineering and Al to solve modern data challenges.

2023

DataSkate is born, embracing sustainability and scalability as core principles while becoming a trusted MuleSoft integration partner.
